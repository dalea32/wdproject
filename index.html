About This Website

This is a college project. In this website, I am going to display a tourist attraction in each of the four provinces of Ireland. I will be examining tourist sites at Achill Island, the Giants Causeway, Kilkenny castle and the Cliffs of Moher. This website will be used mainly for tourists who are looking for new and interesting places to visit throughout Ireland and hopefully this website will be of some use to them
